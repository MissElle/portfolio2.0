~Secret Bio about Elle~

Ultimately, I have a lot of passion in what I do. Growing up having less made me a person who wants to give more. That is why I hold myself to the following before taking on any project:

Is it true?
Is it kind?
Is it necessary?

Whomever first said this was wise. Not everything in this world will be pleasant. If you are asking me to take on a project that does not fit at least one of these conditions, I will not oblige.
